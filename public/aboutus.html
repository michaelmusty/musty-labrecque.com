Nicole and Michael met in the summer of 2015 on the green in Hanover New Hampshire. After seeing what one green had to offer, they decided to walk to another green across the river in Norwich Vermont. Two days later they decided to walk to Boston Lot Lake, where Nicole broke her toe and then promptly left on a trip to Iceland (to walk around there a bit).

Upon her return, Nicole and Michael decided to continue walking together, galavanting around places in New Hampshire, Massachusetts, Maine, and Virginia. In fact, it was on one of their longer walks that they decided to "hit the trails" together for good.

When they are not walking, Nicole and Michael enjoy a wide variety of sitting activities which center around laughing at each other. TODO
