Location

We will be getting married at Mountain Meadows Lodge (MML) in Killington Vermont the weekend of September 22, 2018. We chose this location so that our family and friends could enjoy a weekend together. It is our hope that guests will be able to spend the entire weekend with us, but we understand schedules are busy. If you cannot attend everything that is perfectly fine!

Accommodations

There is a wide variety of places to stay in and around the Killington area. Of course you are welcome to attend the wedding and reception without booking a place to stay overnight, but since most people are traveling from a distance, we have included some information about places to stay below.

If you are staying Friday and Saturday night, then the closest place to stay (that we know of) is the Greenbriar Inn (see below) and we suggest staying there. If you are just planning to stay Saturday night, then the Hampton Inn in Rutland is a good hotel option (we originally suggested the Best Western but it appears that they do not have any more rooms available). Turn Of River Lodge is another option. We will try to keep information for at least one hotel with available rooms on this webpage.

Another option is to simply find a place. There are many choices including hostels/camping, Airbnb/VRBO, and hotels, but be sure to find a place relatively soon!

Children

There have been some inquiries about bringing children to our wedding. We did not include children in the head count, so we may not have room for a large number of children at the reception dinner (we will have a better idea about this after we have all the RSVPs on August 10th). If you want to bring children please indicate this on your RSVP or get in touch with us directly. We may have room for some children at dinner. In the case that there is not room for children at the dinner, you will need to arrange for a sitter during this time. This can be done directly through MML (see http://vermont-wedding.com/support/ for more information about sitters).
